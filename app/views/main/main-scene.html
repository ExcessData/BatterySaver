Airplane Mode
Airplane Mode turns off the cellular and WiFi radios in your phone. This allows your phone to get a good night's sleep, just like you. Keep in mind that you will not receive phone calls while in airplane mode.
Battery Saver is currently turned off. Set the switch to On when you want it to automatically control airplane mode for you.
